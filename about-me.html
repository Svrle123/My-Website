Becoming a Maintenance Electrician

I went to highschool to become a electrotechnician, this is where I was introduced to programming for the first time. After finishing highschool I went to the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Computer Science and Information Technology in Osijek to study electro-energetics. I dropped out after the first semester seeing as it was not for me and in the meantime got a job. A job that would last for 5 years, in which I became very good and mentored two new electricians. I quit the job not because it was bad or hard, but because I had a passion for programming which I wanted to puruse.

Getting into 3D Modeling

I'm always fascinated by new technologies and 3D printing really caught my eye, since it became cheaper to get into the hobby. I started off printing the simple things and testing the limits of my printer the "Ender 3 - Pro". After some time I got into modeling, using programs like Blender and Fusion 360 and really started to like the process of making whatever you can think of. I made alot of models just for fun, but I also made models for others when they needed something that was expensive but I knew I could make in 4 hours for a 10th of the cost. My proudest point is making models for the company I worked at and adding them to a new million dollar machine.
